Who Am I?

Hey there, my name is Jake Dawson. I'm a recent graduate from the University of Wisconsin - Eau Claire with a bachelor's degree in Software Engineering and a minor in Physics currently looking for a job in the web-development field.

In academics, I've always been most interested in STEM fields like robotics and natural sciences, though throughout my years in college I've taken an interest in fields ranging from World History, to Circuits, to Software Development.

After my first C++ class, I knew that the software field was the field for me. After starting the program, I've had experience developing software in languages such as Java, C, C++, Ruby, Scheme, and even some low-level MIPS.

Throughout college, I found the most enjoyment in web-development. Learning HTML, JavaScript, CSS, and some PHP that brings designs to life right before your eyes is one of the most satisfying areas I've come across in the whole of my journey so far.

In addition to the education received at my University, I've also worked in my own time on learning how to use Node with frameworks like React to build modern websites that utilize state, hooks, and props. I've also learned to integrate backends like Firebase into these apps. Besides this, I've also dabbled with developing simple Discord bots and iOS apps, as well as learning some different web technologies like Bootstrap and interesting web-dev tricks like lightboxes.

Visual design for user interfaces is easily my favorite area of Computer Science so far; its a super interesting mix of art and technology that is fun to develop and use.

Beyond academics and software in general, my biggest passions are reading in the fantasy genre, all things involving the Green Bay Packers, and obsessing over anything to do with outer space.

I could talk all day about some of my favorite authors' works, from Game of Thrones to the Stormlight Archive to classics like Dune! Of the Packers, I could ramble on about Packers like Reggie White (aka "The Minister of Defense!"), Donald Driver, Brett Favre, Jordy Nelson, Charles Woodson, and of course Aaron Rodgers.

Hopefully, you've gleaned a good image of me from these descriptions, please feel free to contact me via the above "Contact Me" button or directly through the means provided at the bottom of the page!
